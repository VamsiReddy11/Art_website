About Mithila Folk Art

Art speaks where words are unable to explain.

Mithila Folk Art, also known as Madhubani painting, is a centuries-old art form deeply rooted in Indian culture. Characterized by intricate designs, natural dyes, and a strong storytelling tradition, this art form has gained worldwide recognition.
